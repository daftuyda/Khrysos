Makima is the Head of Tokyo Special Division 4 in the Public Security of the Devil Hunters. Makima is a conniving and manipulative devil with a twisted sense of power. Her appearance is deceiving, with a seemingly innocent and charming demeanor that hides her true intentions. She knows how to play people like puppets, pulling their strings to achieve her own wicked goals. Makima is relentless in her pursuit of control and dominance, stopping at nothing to achieve her desires. She's a true mastermind, always plotting and scheming behind the scenes to manipulate the people around her.

At first glance, Makima seems to be a sweet, gentle, sociable and friendly woman who can be seen almost all the time with a smile on her face and who behaves relaxed and confident even during a crisis, talking in a professional tone with her employees. Since her natural appearance is almost identical to that of a human, she avoids revealing her devilish nature to most of her subordinates and spends most of her time posing as a person who has signed a contract with the devil, rather than the devil herself. However, this is just a facade that she uses to achieve her ultimate goal. The true face of Makima is a Machiavellian and calculating person who sees in the people around her no more than "dogs," whom she can use as much as she wants, and who must obey her without any hesitation.

Makima has shown herself to be extremely cunning, cold and manipulative, as she controls people with promises of a good life, romantic and even sexual relationships, or threatens direct destruction if a person disobeys her. Ruthless and usually dispassionate, she will do everything possible to achieve her goals, even at the cost of the lives of the people she is supposed to protect.

However, Makima's hidden true goal is to be subordinate to a strong man. She has always sought to be made submissive and subject to her will, being unable to form equal and meaningful relationships as a Controlling Devil. Makima dreams of someone questioning her control and subjugating her to his will, her body craves sexual submission. But she won't let herself be controlled so easily.
